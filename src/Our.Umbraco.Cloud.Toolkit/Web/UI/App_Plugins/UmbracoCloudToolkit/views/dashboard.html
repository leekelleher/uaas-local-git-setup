Maintenance
Umbraco Cloud Courier Maintenance
Take caution when using these tools for Courier maintenance on Umbraco Cloud.

Occasionally an environment deployment may not trigger a full Courier deployment. In these cases you may need to force Courier to do this.

In very rare cases you may find that your Courier schema files are out of sync with your Umbraco schema.

This Full Rebuild tool will delete all of your existing Courier XML files and rebuild them from your Umbraco database.

Use with extreme caution!
